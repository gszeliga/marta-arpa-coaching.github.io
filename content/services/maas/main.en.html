PMaaS Program Management as a Service

Management of complex programs (multi-project, cross-functional, strategic) or stand-alone projects using agile and traditional methodologies.

  • ✓Coordination of multidisciplinary teams
  • ✓Continuous monitoring and agile adaptation
  • ✓Delivery of results with maximum efficiency

CMaaS Change Management as a Service

Comprehensive or modular support to manage the human side of change within organizations.

  • ✓Effective communication strategies
  • ✓Engagement and commitment development
  • ✓Cultural transformation aligned with objectives
  • ✓Facilitation of successful adoption of new processes

Both services can be contracted independently or combined to maximize the success of organizational transformation initiatives.
