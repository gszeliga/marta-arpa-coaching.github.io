PMaaS

Program Management as a Service

Management of complex programs (multi-project, cross-functional, strategic) or stand-alone projects using agile and traditional methodologies.

Team coordination Agile adaptation Maximum efficiency

CMaaS

Change Management as a Service

Comprehensive or modular support to manage the human side of change within organizations.

Effective communication Engagement Cultural transformation Process adoption

Both services can be contracted independently or combined to maximize the success of organizational transformation initiatives.
