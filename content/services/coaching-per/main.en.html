--- date: 2025-09-02 draft: false title: What is Ontological Coaching? ---

Ontological coaching is a revolutionary discipline that goes beyond traditional coaching. It focuses on the being of the person, not just on what they do or have. This methodology delves into how we interpret and construct our reality through three fundamental domains: language, emotions, and embodiment.
Unlike other forms of coaching that focus on specific goals, ontological coaching seeks a holistic transformation that allows people to expand their way of being in the world. It is a journey of self-discovery that reveals new possibilities for action and thought.

Personal Coaching – Holistic Transformation
